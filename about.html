About Us

Founded by CEO Benji Bennett and COO Hassan Niang, our non-profit organization was born from humble beginnings with a shared vision to make a difference in the lives of minority groups. It all started when Benji and Hassan recognized the profound impact that providing a platform for foundational skills could have within minority school systems. Their passion for equity in education drove them to create this organization. Over time, we've forged strategic partnerships with dedicated individuals like Victor, Tecambra, and Felicia, who share our commitment to addressing the pressing issue of foundational skills in minority communities. Together, we've been able to make a significant impact, not only locally but also on a global scale. Our journey is a testament to the power of collaboration and the unwavering belief in the transformative potential of education for all.
